Sandwich!

For this recipe, we are essentially making a delicious grilled cheese sandwich.

Ingredients

  • 2 pieces of white bread
  • cheese
  • Basil

Steps

  1. Start by getting the bread. let's cut it in half.
  2. Put the cheese in the middle. Now put it in the grill.
  3. From there, it's just an assembly job. Wait until it melts, then serve!
  4. Eat!
